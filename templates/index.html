Description of Masters Project

This project is focused on creating a web application that aims to decentralize artificial intelligence (AI). By leveraging the principles of federated learning and blockchain technology, the goal is to allow participants to contribute their data to train a base AI model collaboratively. The MNIST dataset, a foundational dataset in the field of machine learning, serves as the starting point for this initiative. Participants can join the project, contribute their MNIST data for model training, and potentially improve the model's accuracy. Improvements to the model are validated and then recorded on the blockchain, ensuring transparency and integrity in the model's development process.
