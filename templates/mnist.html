Description: The MNIST dataset is a large collection of handwritten digits widely used for training and testing in the field of machine learning. Comprising 60,000 training images and 10,000 test images, this dataset serves as the benchmark for evaluating the performance of image processing systems. The simplicity of the MNIST dataset makes it an ideal starting point for any machine learning project, including ours, which focuses on the decentralized training of AI models. By starting with MNIST, we can establish a foundational model that can later be expanded to more complex datasets and challenges.
